About D4PG

D4PG is a collective space where we share insights, learnings, and have conversations on topics including; Digital justice, Afrofuturism, Data and data privacy, AI and much more. We aim to tap into the collective power of community-based changemaking through technology, democracy, and justice.

Whether you're a data scientist, student, artist, community advocate, parent, attorney, educator or community member, we are here to host these dynamic conversations with you!

This year's theme is, Co-Powering an Emergent Horizon Part II: Reimagining Our Relationship with the Future, inspired by our co-authored article in the Stanford Social Innovation Review.

By being a part of this experience, you will:

  • Learn about resources and tools
  • Gain insight into strategies and models to activate in your communities
  • Expand your network by connecting with others
  • Join conversations about the importance of democracy in technology for protecting our civil liberties, ownership, and privacy rights
