Glitch Lab 2025 Artist Residency

Residency of 3 Artists that Learn About Technology

About the Residency

The Glitch Lab is a new residency by the Edgelands Institute and the NOTICE Coalition that supports three Houston-based artists in developing socially engaged art projects exploring how surveillance technologies—such as student activity monitoring software, AI-powered cameras, and vape detectors—are reshaping education and youth justice.

The residency uses participatory art to spark civic dialogue and amplify student and community voices around themes like carceral technologies, school privatization, and cultural narratives of safety.
